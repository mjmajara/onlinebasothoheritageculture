Basotho Music & Dance

Traditional Music

Basotho music includes vocal songs, traditional instruments like the lesiba (a stringed instrument), and drums. It is an essential part of ceremonies and community events.

Traditional Mangae

Basotho traditional music are energetic, expressive, and often performed during festivals, weddings, and social gatherings. They reflect stories, values, and heritage.

Mokhibo Dance - Performed during celebrations with synchronized footwork.

Lesiba - A traditional stringed instrument producing unique melodic sounds.

Drumming Ceremony - Central to community gatherings and storytelling.

Folk Dance - Showcases traditional attire and rhythmic movements.

Wedding Dance - Celebrates unity and cultural heritage.
